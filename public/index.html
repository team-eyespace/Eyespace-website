About Us


Generally, the elderly and visually impaired people walk with someone by their side. But what if nobody is around to help? We were very concerned about the fact that help may not be always available so we wanted to make a positive impact on the elderly and the visually impaired.

Mission


EyeSpace is committed to delivering accessibility, autonomy and inclusion to the Visually Impaired in a fair and accessible way. This way, we aim to give a new look at the world for the people with more security and practicality in the palm of your hands. We hope to empower the visually impaired to achieve more.


How it works?
